first car

The BMW 3/15 was essentially a rebranded and slightly modified version of the Austin 7, adapted to meet German engineering standards. The car was initially produced at the Eisenach factory, which BMW had acquired as part of its automotive expansion. Its affordability, reliability, and compact design made it popular among drivers in the late 1920s and early 1930s.
